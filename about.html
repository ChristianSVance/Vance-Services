About

Vance Services was founded in spring of 2022. It is a Sole Proprietorship that operates out of Rockville Virginia and is owned and opperated by Christian Vance.

The name Vance Services originated in West Monroe Louisiana in 1962 for Thomas Vance's appliance repair company. Thomas successfully ran that company for over three decades. In 2021 Thomas passed away and his grandson honored him by naming his start up company Vance Services.

The Beautiful thing about Vance Services is that our services include a little bit of everything. From weedeating and tree removal to demos and home renos, and everything in between. We take pride in what we do, and we put the customers as the number one priority.
